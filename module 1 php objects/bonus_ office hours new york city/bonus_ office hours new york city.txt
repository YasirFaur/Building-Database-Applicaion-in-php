New York City Office Hours with Dr. Chuck

Dr. Chuck held a big Office Hours event in New York City at Houston Street Hall. Many students and fans came to meet him in person. Everyone introduced themselves and shared how much they enjoy his courses. People came from different places, including Poland, Ghana, and the local area. Dr. Chuck talked about teaching, programming, and the history of the internet in Africa. He mentioned that Ghana was the first Sub-Saharan country with the internet. The event was friendly and full of energy. He also thanked the mentors who help with the courses. Finally, Dr. Chuck said there will be more Office Hours in the future, and he encouraged everyone to keep learning and programming.
