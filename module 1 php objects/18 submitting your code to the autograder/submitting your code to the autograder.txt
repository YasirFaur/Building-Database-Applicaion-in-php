Submitting Your Code to the Autograder
Some autograders need to check your application over the Internet. Your computer usually blocks incoming connections with a firewall. To allow this safely, you can use NGrok. NGrok makes a temporary tunnel through your firewall so the autograder can reach your local server.

We provide guides and videos for using NGrok on Mac and Windows 10. If you can put your app online with a real domain, you do not need NGrok.
